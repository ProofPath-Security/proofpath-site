Trust & Privacy

We take client confidentiality seriously. Below is our baseline posture for projects. We tailor to your requirements on request.

Security posture (v0.1)

  • Access & storage: Client evidence kept in Google Workspace (your tenant or ours); MFA enforced; encryption at rest/in transit.
  • Confidentiality: Mutual NDA; least‑privilege access; named artifact owners.
  • IR & backups: Incident playbook; versioned deliverables; off‑site backups.
  • Data handling: No client data sent to public AI tools by default; any AI use is opt‑in and logged.
  • Subprocessors (minimal): Google Workspace; booking/meeting tools (listed on request).
  • Retention: Working papers kept 12 months unless your policy requires earlier deletion; immediate purge on request.

Privacy

We collect only basic contact details and project metadata. No tracking pixels. For privacy requests, email privacy@proofpath.com.
